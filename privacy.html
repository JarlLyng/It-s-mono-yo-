Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Introduction

"It's mono, yo!" ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information or data when you use our macOS application.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect any personal data. The "It's mono, yo!" application operates entirely locally on your macOS device.

  • We do not track your usage of the app.
  • We do not collect any personal information (such as name, email, or IP address).
  • We do not use any third-party analytics services.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies in the app.

Local File Processing

All audio file conversions are performed locally on your Mac using macOS system frameworks (AVFoundation). Your audio files are never uploaded to any server or cloud service. They remain on your device at all times.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
