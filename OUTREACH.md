## Tips

- Lead with the user problem (left-channel-only on Digitakt is a great hook)
- Offer a **promo code** to creators if asked
- Keep emails **under 150 words**
- Don't follow up more than once
- Track who responds in a simple list (or as GitHub Issues with `outreach` label)
- The best time to email is Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM their local time

---

## What counts as success

- **Coverage**: A blog post or video mention = dofollow backlink + traffic spike
- **Newsletter mention**: Smaller burst, fewer backlinks but very targeted
- **Manufacturer listing**: Best long-term value (e.g. linked from Erica Synths Sample Drum docs)
- **Even a "no"**: At least the editor now knows you exist, which helps later
